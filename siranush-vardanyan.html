Siranush Vardanyan

Yerevan, Armenia

Siranush Vardanyan was born on February 5, 1998, in Armenia. She graduated from Terlemezian Yerevan State College of Arts in 2018 and has been a student at Yerevan Academy of Fine Arts since 2018. As an artist, she has taken part in various exhibitions and competitions such as " Hogevor Veracnund ", "Erek patmutun" " Hayrenik" "Mi ktor krak " etc . Also she has taken part in the project implemented by the organization " A piece of fire " and has won many prizes.
