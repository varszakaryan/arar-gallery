Tigran Ghazaryan

Yerevan, Armenia

Tigran Ghazaryan (b. 1967, Yerevan, Armenia) is a contemporary painter whose creative journey spans over three decades. He trained at the H. Kodjoyan Art School and later at P. Terlemezyan Arts College in Yerevan, grounding his practice in rigorous artistic study. Ghazaryan's work has been featured widely in national and international group exhibitions — including prestigious venues such as the Centre Pompidou in Paris and The Solomon R. Guggenheim Foundation in New York — as well as biennales and cultural showcases across Europe, Russia, the USA, and the Caucasus. He has also presented solo exhibitions in Yerevan and participated in recent group shows and art fairs in Switzerland, where his work continues to engage audiences with its expressive depth and evolving aesthetic. Ghazaryan's art reflects a sustained commitment to painting, and his presence in global exhibitions highlights his contribution to contemporary Armenian art today.
