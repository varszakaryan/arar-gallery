Raydan Zebian (Spazuno)

Beirut, Lebanon

Spaz is a Lebanese muralist who has been actively painting for over a decade. Rooted in the vibrant streets of his homeland, his murals explore the delicate space between reality and dream, often portraying hypnagogic moments those liminal states between wakefulness and sleep where time feels suspended and raw emotion surfaces. A multidisciplinary artist, Spaz moves fluidly across styles, mediums, and collaborations, consistently pushing the boundaries of visual expression. His work has gained international recognition through participation in major festivals such as Meeting of Styles (Germany), Street of Styles (Brazil), and Sikka Festival (Dubai), among others across Europe and Latin America. Beyond their visual impact, his murals serve as a living archive of contemporary society. From Beirut to the world, his art continues to unfold on walls worldwide, alive, shifting, and deeply personal.
