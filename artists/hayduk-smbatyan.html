Hayduk Smbatyan

Etchmiadzin, Armenia

Hayduk Smbatyan (b. 1990, Etchmiadzin, Armenia) is a contemporary Armenian painter known for his figurative, landscape and abstract works. He studied at the Yerevan State College of Fine Arts named after Panos Terlemezian and at the Yerevan State Academy of Fine Arts (BA, MFA). Smbatyan is a member of the Artists' Union of Armenia and has exhibited in solo and group shows such as Gravity at the Union of Architects of Armenia and other exhibitions in Yerevan and abroad. His work has appeared in auctions and is collected internationally.
