Ashot Harutyunyan

Yerevan, Armenia

In spite of having academic education in sculpture, the main inspiration of Ashot's artwork is the Soviet industrial iron. He is using this material as he thinks that it has a unique "historical code". His latest artworks are a combination of an old industrial iron and new stainless still.
